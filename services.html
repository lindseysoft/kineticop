Our Services

What We Provide

Comprehensive Orthotic & Prosthetic Care

Along with excellent patient care, we provide the following Orthotic and Prosthetic services tailored to your individual needs.

Custom Solutions

Orthotics

We provide a comprehensive range of custom orthotic devices designed to support, align, and improve the function of your body. Each device is carefully crafted to meet your specific needs.

  • Spinal — TLSO and LSO bracing for spinal support and stabilization
  • Cervical — Collars, Halo devices, and Cranial Helmets
  • Lower Extremity — AFO, KAFO, HKAFO, CROW, Cam Boot, PRAFO, Hip Abduction Splint, Fracture Brace
  • Upper Extremity — Fracture braces, Wrist and Hand orthoses, Myopro®
  • Footwear — Orthopedic shoes, Diabetic shoes, Custom inserts, Offloading shoes
[Orthotics Image]

Restoring Mobility

Prosthetics

Our prosthetic services are designed to restore your mobility and independence. We work closely with you to create prosthetic solutions that fit your lifestyle and goals.

  • Lower Extremity — Below Knee Prosthesis, Above Knee Prosthesis, Prosthetic Supplies
  • Upper Extremity — Below Elbow Prosthesis, Above Elbow Prosthesis, Hand Prosthesis, Finger Prosthesis, Prosthetic Supplies
  • Mastectomy — Breast Prosthesis, Bras, and related supplies
[Prosthetics Image]

Medicare Covered

Diabetic Footwear Program

Our diabetic footwear program helps protect your feet and prevent complications. Medicare covers diabetic shoes and custom inserts for qualifying patients with diabetes and related foot conditions.

  • Diabetic Shoes — Extra-depth therapeutic footwear designed for diabetic patients
  • Custom Inserts — Molded inserts for pressure relief and support
  • Offloading Shoes — Specialized footwear for wound healing and pressure management
  • Orthopedic Shoes — Supportive footwear for a variety of foot conditions

Multi-Disciplinary Care

Riverside Prosthetic & Orthotic Clinic

The prosthetic clinic was created with collaboration in mind. The benefits of a multi-disciplinary approach to prosthetic care will get you to the next step without the various office visits required to get you ambulatory.

The clinic brings doctors, therapists, orthotists and prosthetists together to foster a collaborative environment that promotes your independence. Held the second Wednesday of every month at Riverside Medical Center in Kankakee.
