What to Expect

Your First Visit

We want your first experience with Kinetic O&P to be smooth and comfortable. Here's what to expect.

1

Schedule

Call us or use our contact form to schedule your initial evaluation. We'll confirm your insurance coverage.

2

Paperwork

Download and complete your patient forms ahead of time, or arrive 15 minutes early to fill them out.

3

Evaluation

Meet with your practitioner for a thorough evaluation. We'll discuss your needs, goals, and create a care plan.

4

Treatment

Custom fabrication and fitting of your device, with follow-up visits to ensure the perfect fit and function.

Be Prepared

What to Bring

Having these items ready will help make your appointment as efficient as possible.

  • ✓ Photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
  • ✓ Insurance card(s) — primary and secondary
  • ✓ Physician's prescription or referral
  • ✓ Completed patient intake forms (if pre-filled)
  • ✓ Any current orthotic/prosthetic devices you use
